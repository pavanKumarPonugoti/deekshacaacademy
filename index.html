ABOUT US


CA. M. Krishna Chaitanya and CA. M. Mallikarjuna Reddy are the founders and Directors of Deeksha CA Academy. When they were in C.A - CPT they recognised the need for a good coaching institute for CA in Nellore where all the subjects can be taught under one roof by Qualified CA's Only. All of them qualified C.A - CPT in the first attempt and started teaching all the subjects of C.A- CPT. Thus, they entered into teaching profession at such an early age. As the strength of students started increasing, they started a coaching centre at Nellore with just 15 students only. In 2011 both of them established Deeksha. In the beginning, they conducted the classes in a small campus located at Wahabpet, Nellore with the strength of just 10 Students and immediately the strength turned about triple because of their relentless efforts.
By finding the need of establishing junior college, exclusively for CA oriented branches like MEC & CEC, they have established junior college too with eminent faculty of Nellore and proved themselves what they are, by securing Seven 10/10 GPA in the 1st batch itself. Now there are Hundreds of Students studying here even though there is a high demand for Science Courses.
